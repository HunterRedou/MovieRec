
Hello, this is MovieRec your Number One Movie Recommendation Programm.

We have sorted out Movies to your liking with simple Commends you can acess them.

The Commands that you can choose are:

old - To show the oldest Movies in our Dataset.

new - To show the newest Movies in our Dataset.

desi - To show the desicription of a Title.

genre - To show the best Movies out of that Genre.

best - To show the best Movies in our Dataset.

long - To show how much runtime a Movie has.

welcome - To show these Commands again.

end - To end the Programm.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________

This is what the Program can do. It read out of a dataset from Kaggle with 13000 Movies.
And provides the the Options that are in the Welcome screen.
